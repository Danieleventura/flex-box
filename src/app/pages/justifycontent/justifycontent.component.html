Justify-content

Justify-content posiciona os itens flex ao longo do eixo principal do container.

Nos exemplos, vamos utilizar o eixo principal do flex-direction: row.

1 - justify-content: flex-start;

2 - justify-content: flex-end;

3 - justify-content: center;

4 - justify-content: space-between;

5 - justify-content: space-around;
